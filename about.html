About Industry Visit Management System

  • Welcome to the Industry Visit Management System, a comprehensive solution designed to streamline and optimize the process of organizing and managing industry visits for educational institutions and corporate entities.

    • Our Mission

  • Our mission is to provide a user-friendly platform that facilitates the planning, coordination, and execution of industry visits. We aim to bridge the gap between academic institutions and industries, fostering meaningful connections and enhancing the learning experience for students.

    • Key Features

    • Efficient scheduling of industry visits.
    • Easy registration and management of participants.
    • Automated communication and reminders.
    • Detailed information about visited industries.
    • Feedback and evaluation mechanisms.

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions, feedback, or inquiries, feel free to contact us at bisag-n.gov.in.

    Thank you for choosing the Industry Visit Management System. We look forward to contributing to the success of your educational and professional endeavors.
